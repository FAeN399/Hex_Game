📖 Introduction
Welcome to the HexCard Forge! This is where you'll combine cards to create powerful synergies.

The forge has 6 sockets arranged in a hexagonal pattern around a central eye. Each socket can hold one card.
💡 Look at the hexagonal grid in the center. These are your 6 forge sockets!
🎴 Select Your First Card
Let's start by selecting a card from your inventory below. Each card has a name, type, and power value.

Click on the Fire Knight card to select it.
💡 Selected cards will be highlighted in orange. Look for the Fire Knight in your inventory!
⚡ Place in a Socket
Great! Now you have a card selected. Let's place it in Socket 1 (the top socket).

Click on the highlighted socket to place your Fire Knight card.
💡 The socket should be glowing blue to indicate it's ready to receive your card.
📊 Watch Your Stats
Excellent! Notice how your stats updated when you placed the card. Your Total Power increased and Cards Placed went to 1.

Let's add another card to see how connections work.
💡 Check the stats panel below the forge to see your current progress.
🔗 Create Connections
Now select the Ice Mage card and place it in Socket 2 (top-left socket) to create your first connection.

Adjacent cards create connections that boost your power!
💡 Cards placed next to each other will show green connection lines between them.
🚀 Build Your Combo
Perfect! You can see the green connection line between your cards. Your multiplier has increased!

Try placing more cards to create additional connections and boost your power even higher.
💡 The more connections you create, the higher your power multiplier becomes!
🎯 Master Forger
Congratulations! You've learned the basics of HexCard Forge:

• Select cards from your inventory
• Place them in forge sockets
• Create connections for bonus power
• Watch your stats grow!
